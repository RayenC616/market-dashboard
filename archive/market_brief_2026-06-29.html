Executive Summary

U.S. equities posted broad gains on Monday, June 29, snapping a five-session losing streak. The S&P 500 rose +1.18% to close at 7,440.43, the Nasdaq Composite surged +2.07% to 25,820.14 — its strongest single-day advance in two weeks — and the Dow Jones Industrial Average climbed +0.59% to a fresh record close above 52,000 for the first time. Tech mega-caps led the advance: five of the Magnificent Seven finished higher, with Tesla +8.5%, Alphabet (debuting in the Dow) +4.5%, Amazon +3%, and SpaceX (SPCX) +7% on Nasdaq-100 inclusion news.
